Get to know me

I am an artist, an illustrator and a junior web designer. I have been drawings since before I could walk and I had to channel my energy and creativity somewhere and I decided to get involved with web and graphic designer and illustrations.

I graduated a language school in Dobrich. Then when I moved to Sofia I knew that I wanted to learn more about design and eventually work in the industry. I had an internship learning all of the essentials regarding web design and worked as a web designer for a few months. I constantly work on new projects and try to improve my skills whenever I can.

Web design is a perfect mixture between creativity and psychology and in order to deliver a great user experience you have to balance both. Of course I am eager to learn more and more and if you find me an asset download my CV and contact me :))
